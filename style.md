### Feedback and Performance
* Giving Feedback - In the moment. Postive: behaviour and impact. Negative: behaviour -> impact -> request or coaching -> outcome
* Receiving Feedback - Short + frequent for positive, and immediate + behaviour focused when negative
* My ideal performance review in either direction is not news to anyone, just an aggregation of what we've already discussed before

### Communication and Influence
* Slack - I love small frequent signals of acknowledgement (emoji's or words work, silence doesn't)
* Flexibility - I change my opinion and my plan easily from data/reasoning
* I love teaching, and tend towards analogies and metaphors to clarify
* I prefer visual communication over long prose

### Learning and Memory
* I learn via experimenting - forgiveness, not permission
* I'll often do something manually the first time to really understand it, then apply automation/reusability afterwards for efficiency
* My brain is a graph - random facts fall out, connections are what stick
