### My Values summed up
Learning -> Freedom -> Adaptability -> Calm
* Learning and Teaching: my primary source of fun and mental health, whether at work or play
* Freedom: the purpose of money is the freedom to act how and when is valuable to you
* Adaptability: I prefer adapting over resisting, and all my opinions are loosely held
* Calm, No Drama: I hate pointless stress, and filter processes and behaviours to maintain it

### My Priorities summed up
Family -> Health -> Security
* Family: my family can always interrupt my work
* Health: physical via daily habits, mental via learning and flexibility and connection
* Security: The money and safety and predictability needed for everything

Work fits across all 3, providing the learning, connection, and financial security me and my family need.
